What We Do

Trusted Aquatic Management Solutions

With over three decades of experience in Alberta, Seahorse Aquatic Vegetation Harvesting offers comprehensive solutions for managing aquatic vegetation. We excel in aquatic weed cutting, dredging, excavating, vegetation removal, and cattail removal. Our services are trusted by private individuals and municipalities throughout the Prairie Provinces and British Columbia.

Our Equipment

  • Truxor amphibious machine DM 5045, T-30 and T-50.
  • Custom designed cutting machines that are environmentally friendly.
  • Compact equipment allows us to cut close to shore and around docks and smaller areas.
  • Adjustable blades for meeting desired cutting depths.
  • Collection baskets and vacuum truck provide safe and fast removal of debris.
